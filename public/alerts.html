How Alerts Work

Dex Alerts

Dex Alerts monitor token payments on DexScreener. When you add a token mint address, the system will track payment statuses for that token and notify you when payments are processed, approved, or cancelled.

Token Launch Alerts

Token Launch Alerts monitor new token launches and match them against your criteria. When a new token matches your alert conditions, it will appear in the Launch Alerts section.

Developer Marketcap

Track developers who currently have a token at a specific marketcap value. For example, setting "$100K" will alert you when a developer who has a token currently valued at $100K launches a new token. This helps identify developers with proven success.

Contains Phrase

Search for tokens with specific words in their name or ticker. For example, searching for "house" will match tokens like "House Token", "DogHouse", "House of Memes", etc. Perfect for finding tokens related to specific themes or keywords.
