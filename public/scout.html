How Scout Works

Sorting Methods

Standard sorting methods:

Most Recent, Highest Marketcap, and Total Volume.


IncrementalVolume Sorting:

Sorts tokens by trading volume in the specified time window. Useful for finding tokens with recent activity. Volume-based sorts automatically refresh every 5 seconds to keep the list current.

Alert Sounds

When a token completes its migration or when Dex payment statuses change, you'll hear alert sounds. The volume can be adjusted in Settings using the "Alert Sound Volume" slider. Positive sounds play for completed migrations and approved payments, while negative sounds indicate cancelled payments.

Dex Icons

Dex payment badges are on the top left of the token card.

⏳: Dex payment processing.

✓: Dex successfully paid.

✗: Dex payment cancelled.
