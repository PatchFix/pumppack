Roadmap

Tokenomics

Tokengating or subscription fees (which can be used for automated buyback and burn) will be used for future features. The first addition will be getting Token Alerts directly to TG or Discord.

I've left certain features/ideas out for the time being to keep my costs low, and see which projects the community is most interested in.

Fee Usage

Fees will be used to pay dex and the like, but I also have specific upgrades in mind you'll find on the next page. It's important to note that these are not set in stone, and will be adjusted based on community feedback.

Early Fee Usage

Dex Payment

Payment for Dex services.

Upgraded Helius RPC

More types and total amount of solana queries, auto-distro of rewards, buy-back and burn capabilities.

Upgraded Heroku Hosting

Allow for more complex projects and web-scraping things like PumpFun stream chats.

PumpSwap Websocket Fees

At launch only unbonded tokens have real time tracking, a low cost upgrade.

Later Upgrades

Virtual Machine(s)

24/7 live stream with chat interaction.

Geyser Websocket

Expand beyond PumpFun tokens, or be able to poll more data types reliably.

Mumble Servers

Twitter API
